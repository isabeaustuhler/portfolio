About Me

In 1995 I was born in Filderstadt (Germany). The first six years of my life i grew up in France, in a small town near Strasbourg. Through the creative professions of my mother, as a dressmaker and my father as a model maker and carpenter, my passion has always belonged to design, drawing and creativity, as well as the craft work. My school degree for technical product design, already enriched me with many basics and beyond. In addition, it has strengthened me in my decision of my professional life. Through my versatile studies Integrated Product Design, I was able to expand these basics and learn and manifest further skills in many different areas.

(Product) Design means to me, the combination of form and function to transform objects into more efficient, aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly artifacts. As well as the creative integration of innovative and sustainable materials.
